How to connect

  1. Download and install smartCARS Central from TFDi Design.
  2. Add Wings of Canada from the community list.
  3. Sign in with the username and connection token (or your AOC password) shown above.
  4. Pick a flight from the schedule, fly it, and your PIREP is filed here for review automatically.

smartCARS-filed flights arrive as pending and count toward your totals once an administrator accepts them.
